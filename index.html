About Us

Welcome to Signature by Noor, where beauty meets elegance and luxury. Our salon is dedicated to providing exceptional beauty services tailored to enhance your natural radiance. With a passion for perfection and an eye for detail, we create personalized experiences that leave you feeling confident and glamorous.

  • Hair Services From precision haircuts and stunning color transformations to luxurious treatments and bridal hair styling, our expert stylists use top-quality products to give you the perfect look.
  • Bridal & Special Occasion Makeup Every bride deserves to look and feel flawless on her big day. Our professional makeup artists specialize in creating timeless, camera-ready bridal looks, along with glamorous makeup for engagements, parties, and photoshoots.
  • Skin Treatments Revitalize your skin with our advanced facials, acne treatments, brightening therapies, and anti-aging solutions. We use premium skincare products to ensure a glowing, healthy complexion.

At Signature by Noor, we believe in delivering excellence with a touch of sophistication. Step into our serene sanctuary and let us redefine your beauty experience.
